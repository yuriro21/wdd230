Los Pinos

Cordero is surrounded by pine trees that at certain times of the year drop their leaves and form an orange carpet where people can go for a picnic, share with the family, take photos or connect with nature.

Plaza Bolivar

The rather popular festivities or events are the "Vuelta en trompo", held every Easter, the "Kite Contest" that takes place at the end of August and the "Old Years Contest", which takes place the last December days in the Campo Deportivo community. The main celebrations of a religious nature are those that have to do with Holy Week and December festivities, such as the "Misas de aguinaldos" and the crowded "Desfile de reyes magos".
